Rice and Chicken

This is a recipe for gymrats who love rice and chciken after a long and hard gym session

It's probably the most common meal for every meathead out there


Ingredients for two servings

  • 3 bags of Basmati Rice
  • 0,5kg of chicken breast
  • frozen peas, sliced carrots and corn
  • seasoning of your choice

Instructions

  1. Cut the chicken into small pieces with a kinfe or scissors
  2. Put your Basmati rice in the boiling water for 10 minutes
  3. Cook the chicken on a frying pan, add your seasoning
  4. Add frozen ingredients to the chicken and cook on low heat for 5 minutes
  5. Add rice and mix them togheter
